<section>
  <h2>Research Interests</h2>
  <p>
    My research focuses on the design and synthesis of chiral organic materials with tailored optoelectronic and chiroptical properties. I am particularly interested in exploring circularly polarized luminescence (CPL) and its applications in advanced devices such as CP-OLEDs. A key aspect of my work involves encapsulation of functional molecules within supramolecular cages to modulate their optical responses and investigate structure–property relationships. I also study the impact of heavy atoms and molecular architecture on chiroptical behavior, aiming to develop novel materials with tunable optical activity for emerging photonic and electronic applications.
  </p>
</section>
